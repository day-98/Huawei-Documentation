
### Tasks

1. Log in to the iMaster NCE-Campus service plane as the admin user.
2. Choose System > User Management > License Management on the home page.
3. Click Obtain ESN.
4. In the Obtain ESN dialog box, record the generated ESN to apply for a license.
5. Click Copy to collect ESN.
